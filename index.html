UW Challenge

Projects from the Data Crunch for Social Good event

View on GitHub

Objective

Enable social innovators to address childhood hunger and access to fresh, nutritious food in Wake, Durham, Orange and Johnston county

Ideas

We captured some of the ideas that were discussed during the ideation process in the Wiki

Projects

To support the United Way of the Greater Triangle, the teams worked on the following projects:

  • UWchallenge: Data - Where we are and where we need to go (PDF)
  • Bus accessibility of food pantries
  • Measuring the distance people in need travel to food pantries (PDF)
  • Childhood Hunger and Summer Feeding Sites (PDF)
  • Getting NC produce to people in need (PDF)
  • NC Food Resource Exploration Map (Shiny App)
  • Poverty Demographics and Food Assistance Locations (PDF)
  • Estimating food waste of grocery stores in Raleigh (PDF)
