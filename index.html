Innovate United

2016 Childhood Hunger Challenge

Objective

To support the United Way of the Greater Triangle's 2016 Childhood Hunger Challenge, the NC Data4Good group organized the DataCrunch for Social Good event. The main objective is to enable social innovators to address childhood hunger and access to fresh, nutritious food in Wake, Durham, Orange and Johnston county

Data Sets

Data sets cleaned and scrubbed by Monika Sanghi, Zeydy Ortiz, Lucia Gjeltema, Tom Robinson and Mark Hutchinson

  • Farmer's Markets - Export.csv
  • Grocery Stores - cleandata_grocery_store.csv
  • SNAP Retailers - SNAP-NC.csv
  • Food Banks - food_bank-NC.csv
  • Food Pantries - pantries.csv
  • Bus Stops - local_agencies.csv, busstops.rds
  • Schools - NCschools-info.csv, local_school_perf1314.csv, local_school_perf1415.csv
  • American Community Survey (US Census) - Census-ACS
  • Shapefile for Census Tracts (US Census) - census tract shapefile.zip
  • Shapefile for Public School Zoning (US Census) - unified (elementary and secondary) school district zoning shapefile.zip
  • Places in NC - NCdata.csv
  • Feeding Sites - available at the event
  • Active Agency Programs - agency_programs.csv, agency_programs_cleaned.csv
  • FIPS codes for NC counties - fips_counties.csv
  • Food Bank CENC counties and locations - FoodbankCENC_counties.csv, FoodbankCENC_locations.csv
  • Area Health Resources File (AHRF) - ahrf_headers_parsed.csv, ahrf_parsed.csv
